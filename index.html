About

An online voting system offers several key benefits that make the election process more efficient and accessible. It allows voters to cast their ballots conveniently from anywhere with internet access, which is especially helpful for people with disabilities, those living in remote areas, or citizens residing abroad. The system can significantly reduce the costs associated with traditional voting methods by minimizing the need for physical ballots, polling stations, and on-site staff. It also enables faster vote counting and result reporting, improving overall efficiency. Additionally, the ease of use and accessibility can lead to increased voter turnout, while also reducing the environmental impact by limiting paper usage and transportation needs.
